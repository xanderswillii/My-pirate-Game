Alberto's Quest
A 3D pirate action game where you play as alberto a beared pirate on quest for treasure and adventure
missing his left hand, Alberto wears a sharop crochet hook in its place, and he is never seen without his loyal bird compagnon perched upon it.

Gameplay
Take control of Alberto and explore the pirate world and battle enemies with his swords battling his enemies and navigating an island.

Features
3D movement:  Walk, run and navigate the world with smooth responsive controls
Combat System: Engage enemies with sword attacks using a state-machine-driven combat system
Camera: Dynamic gimbal camera that rotates and tilts around the player
Animation System — Fluid animations for idle, walking, running, jumping and sword attacks
